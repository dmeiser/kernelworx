# Kernelworx — Brand Guide

*Built for parents and Units. Use it on your own or with the whole pack.*

Kernelworx helps parents of Scouting America Scouts manage their Scout's popcorn sales. Volunteer-run, open-source (MIT). Live at https://kernelworx.app/

Extracted from designmd://kernelworx-is-a-website.

## Color roles

- **Background Gray** (`#f5f5f5`) — background: page canvas (MUI background.default)
- **Text Primary** (`#333333`) — foreground: body text and headings
- **Primary Blue** (`#1976d2`) — accent: primary actions, links, brand emphasis (dark #1565c0, light #42a5f5)
- **White / Paper** (`#ffffff`) — surface: cards, panels, dialogs (MUI background.paper)
- **Text Secondary** (`#666666`) — muted: secondary text and metadata
- **Border Gray** (`#e0e0e0`) — border: rules, dividers, card outlines

## Typography

- Display: Bricolage Grotesque
- Body: Atkinson Hyperlegible
