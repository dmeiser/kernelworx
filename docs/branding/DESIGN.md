# Kernelworx

> Category: Brands

> Surface: web

*Built for parents and Units. Use it on your own or with the whole pack.*

Kernelworx helps parents of Scouting America Scouts manage their Scout's popcorn sales. Volunteer-run, open-source (MIT). Live at https://kernelworx.app/

## Color Palette

| Role | Name | Hex | Usage |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| background | Background Gray | `#f5f5f5` | page canvas (MUI background.default) |
| foreground | Text Primary | `#333333` | body text and headings |
| accent | Primary Blue | `#1976d2` | primary actions, links, brand emphasis (dark #1565c0, light #42a5f5) |
| surface | White / Paper | `#ffffff` | cards, panels, dialogs (MUI background.paper) |
| muted | Text Secondary | `#666666` | secondary text and metadata |
